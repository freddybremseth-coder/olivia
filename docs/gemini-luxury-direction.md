# Gemini Luxury Direction Applied

The Doña Anna premium oil line should feel closer to niche perfume, luxury cosmetics and high-end wine than rustic farm food.

## Visual codes

- Matte black bottle or very dark UV glass.
- Narrow vertical label.
- Thick textured white cotton paper.
- Brushed gold or copper foil.
- Very large negative space.
- Elegant serif wordmark for Doña Anna.
- Minimal sans-serif with wide tracking for product names.

## Logo concepts to test

1. **Anna line art**
   - Single continuous line forming a subtle female profile.
   - Ends in an olive leaf.
   - Artistic, boutique, feminine and premium.

2. **DA monogram**
   - Architectural D + A.
   - Small gold oil drop inside the A.
   - Best for cap embossing, wax seal, foil stamp and premium labels.

3. **Pure wordmark**
   - No symbol, just DOÑA ANNA with strong spacing.
   - Most confident and luxury-oriented.

## How to split oil and table olives

Premium oil can be very minimal and black/white/gold.

Table olives for Spanish markets and restaurants should keep more warmth:

- market seal
- branch icon
- sun/terrace motif
- Spanish words like Aceitunas de Mesa, Biar, Alicante

This gives one brand with two expressions:

- **Doña Anna Alta Gama** for oil
- **Doña Anna Mercado / Mesa** for table olives
