# Doña Anna Brand Family

## Luxury oil line

All premium oil labels use:

- DA monogram as stamp
- pure wordmark as brand anchor
- matte black or dark UV bottle
- narrow textured white label
- brushed gold foil
- QR traceability block

### Verde Vivo
First early harvest. Highest polyphenol focus. 250 ml. Fine-dining finishing oil.

### Verde Alto
Second early harvest. High polyphenol, rounder and more scalable. 250 ml / 500 ml.

### Raíz Antigua
Old-tree estate selection. Limited allocation. Bottle numbering recommended.

### Monovarietal Collection
Genovesa, Gordal, Changlot Real and Picual tasting releases. Best as restaurant education and tasting kits.

### Cocina Viva
Chef format in 2 L / 5 L dark tin or luxury bag-in-box. B2B kitchen oil with traceability.

## Table olives

### Mesa
Warmer Spanish-market expression for table olives, jars, market signage and restaurant starters.

Mesa can use the market seal instead of the ultra-minimal DA monogram when sold in vegetable markets and bars.
